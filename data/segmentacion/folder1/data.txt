*Creating a User with the User Interface**

This document explains how to create a new user account using the graphical user interface (UI).

**Steps:**

1. Open the application.
2. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
3. Enter your desired username and email address.
4. Create a strong password and confirm it.
5. Fill in any additional information required by the form (e.g., name, location).
6. Click on the "Create Account" button.

**Additional Notes:**

* A confirmation email will be sent to your provided address.
